Overview of Project

Project 21-23 would add Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) service between stations in Ellicott City, the county seat of Howard County, and Silver Spring in Montgomery County, with nine intermediate stops. The service would provide connections to the Flash BRT, which scheduled to open on US 29 between Silver Spring and Burtonsville in 2020.
